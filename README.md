# 📊 Business Insights 360 | Power BI Dashboard
A 360 degree view of AtliQ Hardware's business units visualised through a unified Power BI dashboard.

### Project Overview

**Business Insights 360** is an end-to-end **Power BI analytics project** built as a capstone after completing the *Get Job Ready: Power BI Data Analytics for All Levels 3.0* course by Codebasics.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate how **five years of company data** can be transformed into **actionable business insights** across multiple business units using Power BI.

The dashboard is designed for **executive and senior management audiences**, focusing on clarity, storytelling, and decision support rather than technical complexity.

---

## 🏢 Business Case

The project is based on a fictional consumer goods company, **Atliq Hardware**, operating across:

* Finance
* Sales
* Marketing
* Supply Chain
* Exceutive 

Despite strong Net Sales and healthy Gross Margins, the company was experiencing **net losses**. The dashboard helps uncover the underlying drivers behind this performance.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Strong revenue growth and stable gross margins at a consolidated level
* Net losses driven by high marketing costs and excessive discounting
* Customer and product concentration risk impacting profitability
* Forecast inaccuracies leading to excess inventory and stock-out risks
* Clear trade-offs between growth and profitability across regions and segments

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* **Finance View** with full P&L, cost breakdown and profitability analysis
* **Sales View** analysing performance by product, customer and region
* **Marketing View** linking spend to revenue and margins
* **Supply Chain View** covering forecast accuracy, net error and inventory risk
* **Executive View** for top-level performance monitoring

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used

* Power BI Desktop
* Data Modelling (Star Schema)
* DAX for KPIs and calculations
* Business and Executive Reporting
* Data Storytelling and Visual Design
* Microsoft Fabric exposure through migration use case

## 🚀 Key Takeaway

This project highlights that **effective analytics is not about showing more data, but about enabling better business decisions**.
